Pizza rolls can sit out in the fridge for up to four days but eating them
older than that can increase your risk of contracting foodborne illnesses.
They should never sit out longer than two hours.
Similarly, you should not warm up a pizza roll in the microwave
if it has been sitting out for longer than that.
